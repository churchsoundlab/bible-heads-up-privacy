Privacy Policy — Bible Heads Up

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Bible Heads Up ("the app") is a single-device, pass-the-phone family game published by Church Sound Lab. This policy explains how the app handles your information. In short: the app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We do not collect any personal or device information.

Data stored on your device

To let you continue a game and keep your preferences, the app saves the following only on your device (via Android app storage). This data never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else:

  • Game progress (scores, current team/round, timer state, which cards are left in the round)
  • Settings (team names, round length, tilt sensitivity, skips per round, win condition, sound, tilt-direction, and the reset-cards-each-game option)
  • Any custom cards you add
  • A "seen" history of which cards have been used, so cards don't repeat (you can clear this any time from Settings)

You can remove all of this at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Device sensors

Gameplay uses the device's motion sensor (accelerometer) to detect when you tilt the phone down (correct) or up (pass). This reading is used live, on your device, during a round only. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. No runtime permission is required for the accelerometer.

Network use

The app runs fully offline and makes no network requests. It contains no ads and loads no remote content.

Children's privacy

The app is family-friendly and collects no personal information from anyone, including children. Because no data is collected, none is knowingly collected from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
